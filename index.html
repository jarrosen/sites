The Official Rulebook

  • Gameplay Style: The game is similar to American Football, played with a line of scrimmage and a set of downs.
  • Scoring: A touchdown is worth 7 points. A "pick 6" (an interception returned for a touchdown) is a massive, game-changing 20 points. There are no extra points.
  • Contact: The game is strictly two-hand touch. However, blocking and setting picks for your teammates are fully allowed and encouraged.
  • Defense: Any and all defenders are allowed to rush the quarterback on any given play.
  • The Ball: The game is played exclusively with a large, yellow, foam or rubber ball. No other ball is permitted.
  • The Britalian Clause: Once per game, a team can invoke the "Britalian Clause," allowing them to attempt a trick play that would otherwise be illegal (e.g., two forward passes).

Meet the Founders

Britalian Football wasn't born in a boardroom. It was forged on the fields of Pasadena by a group of friends with a love for competition and a passion for the game. What started as a simple backyard pastime quickly evolved into the organized, high-stakes sport we know and love today.
